# Support Policy

This document defines the support policy for Litsxg.

Support is intentionally limited.
There is no obligation to assist beyond documentation.
Use implies acceptance of this policy.

---

## Overview

Litsxg is provided as a finished tool.

There is no formal support channel.
There is no service-level agreement.
There is no guarantee of response.

---

## Supported scope

Support, where offered, is limited to:

- clarification of documented behavior
- correction of documentation errors
- bug reports that align with documented behavior

Support does not extend beyond defined scope.

---

## Unsupported requests

The following requests are unsupported:

- feature requests
- security upgrades
- cryptographic enhancements
- usability improvements
- performance tuning
- operational consulting

Such requests will be ignored or declined.

---

## Bug reports

Bug reports are considered only if:

- the behavior contradicts documentation
- the issue is reproducible
- the report is precise and minimal

Undefined behavior is not a bug.

---

## Security reports

Security reports are handled as follows:

- reports that restate known non-claims are ignored
- reports requesting protections outside scope are declined
- reports identifying defects in documented behavior may be reviewed

No bounty program exists.

---

## Response expectations

There is no response-time guarantee.

Responses may be:
- delayed
- partial
- absent

Silence does not imply acknowledgment.

---

## Operator responsibility

Operators are responsible for:

- reading documentation in full
- operating within defined constraints
- managing their own deployments

Support does not replace responsibility.

---

## Community interaction

Community discussion, if any, is informal.

There is no moderation guarantee.
There is no obligation to engage.

Community interaction does not constitute support.

---

## Support termination

Support may be withdrawn at any time.

No notice is required.
Existing documentation remains authoritative.

---

## Scope enforcement

If a support behavior is not described here, it does not exist.

No implied assistance is provided.

---

## Final statement

Litsxg does not promise support.

The documentation is the primary and final reference.
